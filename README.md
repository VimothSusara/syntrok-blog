# Syntrok Blog

**Write. Read. Discover.**

Syntrok Blog is a production-grade, multi-author publishing platform built for teams and communities that need more than a simple CMS. Authors get a modern writing experience with rich media and AI-assisted workflows; readers get fast discovery through categories, tags, filters, and semantic search; operators get governance tools for users, content, comments, and AI configuration.

## Vision

The platform is designed around three audiences:

- **Authors** — Create and manage posts with a Tiptap editor, cover images, categories, tags, drafts, and publish workflows. AI assists with summarization, SEO metadata, tag suggestions, and future in-editor enhancements — always configurable and auditable.
- **Readers** — Browse a public blog with search, multi-filter discovery (category, tag, author, date), and article pages optimized for reading and sharing.
- **Superadmins** — Operate the system through an admin panel: user lifecycle, comment moderation, taxonomy management, AI provider/model settings, and a full audit trail of sensitive actions.

## Platform principles

- **Trust & governance** — Role-based access, audit logging, moderation, and transparent AI usage.
- **Performance & SEO** — Server-rendered public pages, shareable filter URLs, metadata, and production deployment on Vercel.
- **Extensibility** — Schema and architecture ready for comments, reactions, embeddings/pgvector semantic search, email notifications, and analytics.

## Tech stack

Next.js (App Router) · TypeScript · Tailwind CSS · shadcn/ui · Clerk · Prisma · PostgreSQL (Neon) · Tiptap · Cloudinary · Google Gemini · Vercel
